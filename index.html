The SIGCHI-Italy maintains data on two different activities related to Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) in Italy:

  • A list of HCI Courses in Italy (since 2015);
  • A list of HCI Research Labs in Italy (since 2019).

For both of them, you can search for particular courses or labs. You can also browse an overview on the collected data, including maps and charts.

Are you interested in adding your course or lab? It's as easy as sending us a pull request!
